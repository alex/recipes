Mashed Potatoes
===============

Ingredients
-----------

* 4 medium Idaho or Yukon Gold potatoes
* 3 tablespoons butter
* |1/2| cup hot milk

Instructions
------------

#. Peel potatoes.
#. Cut into 1/16ths or so.
#. Put into pot of water, add some salt.
#. Boil for 20 minutes on high heat.
#. Drain water.
#. Rice potatoes.
#. Add butter and milk.
#. Mix until smooth.
