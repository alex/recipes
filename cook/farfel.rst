Farfel
======

Ingredients
-----------

* |1/3| cup farfel
* salt

Instructions
------------

#. Fill 1-quart pot half way with water.
#. Add salt and farfel to pot.
#. Put on stove at medium-high heat.
#. Once water begins boiling, lower temperature slightly.
#. Cook until soft, approximately 20 minutes.
