Egg Salad
=========

Ingredients
-----------

* 2 eggs
* Mayonaise
* Mustard powder
* Paprika

Instructions
------------

* Place two eggs in a small pot of water (water should cover the eggs).
* Heat on stove at high heat for 20 minutes.
* Peel eggs.
* Slice-and-dice eggs.
* Add a glob of mayonaise (one large spoonful, approximately).
* Add a pinch of mustard powder and paprika.
* Mix with fork.
